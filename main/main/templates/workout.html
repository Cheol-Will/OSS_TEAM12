Pushup


Traditional pushups are beneficial for building upper body strength. They work the triceps, pectoral muscles, and shoulders.
When done with proper form, they can also strengthen the lower back and core by engaging (pulling in) the abdominal muscles.

Squat


They strengthen your lower body, targeting your glutes and quadriceps. They also make you use your core muscles.
Other muscles that benefit from squats are:
Hip muscles, Calves,
Hamstrings, Obliques

Plank


The plank is a bodyweight exercise.
Bodyweight exercises are workouts that you can do with nothing but your own body, which is appealing for a number of reasons.
You don’t need to buy expensive equipment to do bodyweight exercises
